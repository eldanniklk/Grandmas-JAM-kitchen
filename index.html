JAM is the Join-Accumulate Machine. A service receives work packages. Packages contain work items. Refine processes work and produces results. Those results are checked by the protocol flow. Accumulate integrates valid results into the service state. onTransfer handles incoming transfers/messages from other services.

This is an educational simplification. Exact JAM implementation details may evolve.
